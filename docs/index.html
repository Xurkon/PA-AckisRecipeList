Features

📋 Complete Database

Scan your tradeskills and discover every missing recipe with detailed acquisition information for all professions.

🗺️ Map Integration

Automatic TomTom waypoint integration. Click any acquisition source to set a waypoint and navigate directly to it.

🔍 Advanced Filters

Filter by acquisition method, item type, quality, binding, reputation requirements, and more.

💡 Smart Tooltips

Hover over NPCs to see which recipes they teach, sell, or drop. Works for trainers, vendors, and mobs.

📊 Specialties

Full support for profession specializations including Alchemy, Blacksmithing, Engineering, Leatherworking, and Tailoring.

🔄 Alt-Friendly

Track recipes across all your characters. See what your alts know when planning your next craft.

Slash Commands

/arl Open the AckisRecipeList main window
/ackisrecipelist Alternative command to open the main window
Shift + Click Scan Force rescan of current profession
Alt + Click Scan Clear all TomTom waypoints
